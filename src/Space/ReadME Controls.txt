Hyper Space Cowboy Simulator EX Plus Alpha

Goal of the game is to buy low and sell high, and fight pirates for cash.

On ground controls:

Left and right arrow key for movement
space to interact with objects.
	Girl with black and red hair is usually a fuel seller
	Girl with Orange hair is shop keeper
	Guy with pyramid on head is parts/ship seller
	Banker does banking and loans
	Board lets you hire mercenaries
Remember to space on portals to move to the next Screens.

In combat controls:

Left and right move your ship
Space fires your normal laser
"Z" lets you bring up your shield
"X" lets you shoot your laser cannon
If you hire a mercenary, the mercenary will go after the enemy ships and shoot on its own.(Quite OP)
